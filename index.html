Our Courses

Film, Sound & Video [FSV]

FSV is Singapore's top filmmaking course, where aspiring filmmakers tell impactful stories and raise social awareness. From pre-production to post-production, students use industry-standard tools and facilities. They create works that inspire, uplift, and make a difference.

Mass Communication [MCM]

MCM is a premier course that trains versatile media professionals ready to excel in diverse fields. Students gain hands-on experience in journalism, advertising, radio, and video production. It's a dynamic learning journey preparing them for Singapore's vibrant media landscape.

Media Post-Production [MPP]

MPP trains students in motion graphics, audio and video editing, and multi-camera production. They create immersive content using 3D motion graphics, sound design, and projection mapping. Graduates are skilled in crafting for 'live' stage events and dynamic environments.
